Tips for Eating More Plant-Based Meals

  • Start with one plant-based meal a day, like oatmeal or a veggie stir-fry.
  • Experiment with new vegetables and plant proteins such as beans, lentils, and tofu.
  • Swap dairy milk for plant-based alternatives like almond or oat milk.
  • Try meatless versions of your favorite dishes, such as veggie tacos or pasta primavera.
  • Plan your meals ahead to include a variety of colorful fruits and vegetables.
