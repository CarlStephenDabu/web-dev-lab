What is Choose Eco-Friendly Transport?

Choosing "Eco-Friendly Transport" refers to modes of transportation that are less detrimental to the environment than conventional modes that mostly rely on fossil fuels. It's all about providing your travel needs while lowering carbon emissions, saving energy, and promoting sustainable living.

Why is it important?

  • Reduces air pollution and greenhouse gases.
  • Helps fight climate change.
  • Saves money on fuel and car maintenance.
  • Promotes healthier lifestyles through walking and cycling.
  • Builds more sustainable cities for future generations.
