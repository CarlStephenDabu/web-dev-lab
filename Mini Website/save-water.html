Why Save Water?

Water is essential for life, but it is a limited resource. Conserving water helps ensure that everyone has access to clean water, protects ecosystems, and supports sustainable living for future generations.

How You Can Help

Take shorter showers to save gallons daily.
Turn off taps while brushing your teeth.
Use collected rainwater for gardening.
Fix leaks promptly to avoid water waste.
Choose reusable bottles instead of plastic ones.
