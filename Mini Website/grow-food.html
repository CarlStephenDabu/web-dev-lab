Grow Your Own Food — Simple Tips

Start small, enjoy fresh food, and have fun gardening!

  • Start small: try herbs, tomatoes, or lettuce first.
  • Pick a sunny spot (6+ hours of sun).
  • Use good, loose soil.
  • Water in the morning, not too much.
  • Grow what you like to eat.
  • Containers work for small spaces.
  • Compost kitchen scraps.
  • Rotate crops each season.
  • Try companion planting (e.g., basil with tomatoes).
  • Check plants often for water and pests.
